We invest in the software companies that make the modern enterprise indispensable.

Northstone Invest is a Zurich-based investment firm focused on software companies. We invest in businesses with scalable models, recurring revenue and durable competitive advantages. Our emphasis is on software that is business-critical to the enterprises that run it and that creates long-term value. 01 The thesis

Great software businesses compound. We invest where that demand is most durable.

Enterprise software creates lasting value when it becomes indispensable to day-to-day operations. Companies renew that software not out of habit, but because it underpins business-critical processes. These are precisely the business models we look for: recurring revenue, high retention and durable competitive advantages.

01
Durable, recurring revenue

We favour businesses where the customer renews because the software is woven into daily operations — not because switching is merely inconvenient, but because the software creates genuine value.

02
Defensible positions

We invest in companies with durable competitive advantages — through deep integration, proprietary data or business-critical workflows. What matters is not the short-term head start, but the long-term defensibility of the business model.

03
Non-discretionary spend

We favour software whose use is business-critical to the enterprise. Such solutions stay relevant even in demanding economic conditions, because they underpin ongoing operations and cannot easily be replaced.

04
Particular conviction: quality over category

Our investment decisions are guided not by particular software categories, but by the quality of the business. We look for scalable models with durable competitive advantages and long-term value-creation potential.

How we work

A repeatable method, applied the same way to every opportunity — discipline we can describe before any outcome is in.

01
Underwrite the demand

Before any investment, we test that the demand is long-term, recurring and structural — not short-lived or cyclical.

02
The first hundred days

The first hundred days lay the foundation for everything that follows. Together with management, we define the measures with the greatest value-creation potential — from pricing strategy and the product roadmap to key hires, reporting and governance.

03
Compounding the platform

We screen complementary products against a shared data model, integrating what fits the suite and only bringing in companies that strategically strengthen the overall offering.

04
Governance from day one

We treat governance, information security and data protection not as a later stage of maturity, but as an integral part of a professionally run company — from day one.

04 Responsible investing

Good governance is the standard we hold ourselves to.

For us, responsible investing begins with professional company leadership. That is why, even when selecting new investments, we look closely at governance, information security and sustainable corporate structures. Together with management teams, we create the conditions for long-term growth and responsible entrepreneurship.

In diligence

Governance, data protection and security factors are assessed in every decision — integrated, not bolted on after.

In ownership

After investing, we support our portfolio companies in building professional governance structures, transparent reporting and sustainable company leadership.

In impact

Our aim is to develop companies sustainably and to create long-term enterprise value — for customers, employees and shareholders alike.

07 FAQ

Questions, answered.

What is the track record?

Northstone Invest is in the process of building its investment portfolio. As a young investment firm, we do not yet have a long realised track record. Our investment approach rests on a clearly defined strategy, a disciplined selection process and a long-term value-creation philosophy.

How many portfolio companies are there?

The portfolio is being built selectively. We publish information on investments once the respective transactions have closed.

How can I invest alongside Northstone?

If you would like to learn more about Northstone, our investment philosophy or ways of working together, we welcome your enquiry. Further information is provided on request and in line with the applicable regulatory requirements.

Who can invest?

Investments are open to qualified investors — including institutions, family offices and eligible private investors; access follows the eligibility rules of the relevant jurisdiction. For further information about Northstone and possible forms of working together, we welcome your enquiry — in line with the applicable regulatory requirements.

What do you invest in?

Software companies with durable, recurring revenue and defensible positions, across Europe and North America — with particular conviction in the software that regulated industries depend on, where demand is structural rather than cyclical.

What is the typical hold period?

We invest for the long term. The typical hold period for an investment is five to seven years, and may extend beyond that depending on how the company develops.

Where are you based?

Northstone Invest is headquartered in Zurich, Switzerland, and operates under Swiss law with independent administration, external audit and legal counsel. Further information about Northstone is provided on request.

How do I get in touch?

Complete the form on our contact page and we will follow up to answer your enquiry and, where of interest, arrange a call. Further information is provided on request and in line with the applicable regulatory requirements.
